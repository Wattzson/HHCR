We are here to help

Have you finally reached the place in your life where you are ready to have the bondage of addiction broken? Are you tired of the hurt and pain that comes with a life of addiction? Tired of the consequenses of your bad decisions? Are you ready to change but don't know how?

HHCR is a short-term residential environment promoting structure, accountability and community. We encourage residents while equipping them with the spiritual tools for lasting and meaningful change.

Halfway Home Christian Residence (HHCR) is a fully recognized 501 c (3) public charity in Pensacola, Florida, founded by Richard and Katherine Hardy in 2016, to help with the overwhelming need for women’s transitional housing in Escambia county.
